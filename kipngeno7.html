About KIPNGENO CYRENE

Established in Kericho, KIPNGENO CYRENE has been the premier destination for quality beverages in the region. We take pride in offering an extensive selection of both alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks to suit every taste and occasion.

Our commitment to quality, customer service, and community has made us a trusted name in the beverage industry. We source our products from reputable suppliers to ensure you receive only the best.
